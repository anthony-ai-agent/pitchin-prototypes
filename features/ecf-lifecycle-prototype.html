Money invariants this prototype enforces

Checkout — the pledge becomes a wallet hold. Wallet: reserve existing money (LockedAmount ↑, no row). External payment: tops up the wallet (Balance ↑, one Top up credit row) and immediately holds it.
Balance On Hold shows the ECF line for every pledge — all ECF money now sits in the wallet as a real hold, so the On Hold total, Total wallet value and Withdrawable all stay honest (no display-only money).
Refund = release. LockedAmount ↓, Balance =, Available ↑ — money moves from on-hold back to spendable inside the wallet. No transaction row on any path; audit entry only. (Wallet or external, the mechanic is identical.)
Allotment (point of no return) — Balance ↓ and LockedAmount ↓, one Invest debit row. Refund refused once Share Allotment / Success; eligible only in PreLive / Live / Cooling-off.
